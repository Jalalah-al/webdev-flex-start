Information for Students

Explore our state-of-the-art facilities and resources to support your learning journey.

Our Facilities

Our facilities:

  • Advanced Computing Labs: Equipped with the latest hardware and software for programming, cybersecurity, data science, and more. Labs are accessible 24/7 for flexibility.
  • Design Studios: Creative spaces with high-end graphic tablets, software, and collaborative zones for group projects.
  • Innovation and Makerspace: A hub with 3D printers, laser cutters, CNC machines, and prototyping tools for creativity and invention.
  • Technology Sandbox: Explore VR, AR, and AI technologies with cutting-edge devices and software.
  • Collaborative Learning Spaces: Smartboards, video conferencing tools, and flexible seating foster teamwork and creative problem-solving.
  • Library and Resource Centre: Comprehensive library with books, journals, digital resources, and quiet study areas.
  • Student Hub: Social center with a café, lounge areas, and recreational facilities for relaxation and connection.
  • Career and Development Centre: Offers career counseling, resume workshops, and job placement services.
  • Fitness and Wellness Centre: Fully equipped gym, fitness classes, and wellness services for holistic health.
  • On-Campus Housing: Modern residence halls with amenities like high-speed internet, study lounges, and common areas.

Experience Cantor College

Cantor College’s facilities are designed to enhance your learning experience and support your academic and personal growth. We invite you to explore our campus, discover our resources, and make the most of everything we have to offer.
